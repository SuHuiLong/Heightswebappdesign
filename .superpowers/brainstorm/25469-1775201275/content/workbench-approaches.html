What to compare

  • Does the current question stay visually dominant?
  • Can you understand the device / cloud / agent chain at a glance?
  • Does the result area feel generated from the prompt, not like a fixed dashboard?

Known risk

  • Too much chrome at the top makes it look like an observability console.
  • Too much history on the left weakens the result surface.
  • If the right rail becomes the only process view, the system capability story stays hidden.
